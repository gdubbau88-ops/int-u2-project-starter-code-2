Why you should be required to take a test to own a gun


The reason you should be required to take a test to own a gun is due to the high rate of gun violence. The reason this will be effective is because it will help ensure that only responsible individuals who understand the risks and responsibilities associated with gun ownership can obtain them.

Rate of Gun Violence

According to the CDC 48,000 people die from gun violence each year. that is approximately 132 people per day.

Intelligence of offenders

According to a study done by the University of Michigan, offenders who use guns in crimes have an average IQ of 85, which is below the average IQ of 100.

About The Test

The test would be similar to that of the hunters safety test, it would ensure that someone would know gun safety, had no prior criminal record, and was competent to use a firearm responsibly.
